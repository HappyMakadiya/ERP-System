In today’s high competitive business world, management should aim beyond survival, at market leadership. You need all the right tools. DataNote ERP by SAFAL is a complete business solution, that’s in your budget whether you are a small firm or a SME or a giant organization with any given process structure complexity.DataNote ERP by SAFAL is a complete and integrated management suite, covering all your company’s operational needs in terms of production management, distribution, inventory, finance and human resources. DataNote ERP is a multi-company, multi-division and multi-currency solution and can be adapted to suit the specific elements and legal requirements of each country. It also guarantees optimum management of midsize and large companies, on both a national and international scale. Core Modules


Marketing Management
CRM module encompasses functionality for the pre-sales and sales-related activities in an organization. It includes Forecast & Analysis, Team, Campaign, Lead Management, Inquiry / Opportunity, Proposal Making and Sales Order activity. It also covers the follow-up management, which can be sync with Google calendar and DataNote dashboards.

Planning & Production
Manufacturing, Planning and Control Module in DataNote helps to optimize the utilization of manufacturing capacity, material, components, workforce and other resources. Purchase Management
Purchase Management in DataNote ERP streamlines the process of procurement of raw materials, packaging material, sub-assembly and other non-inventory materials. It includes the purchase operations like Indent, RFQ – Request for Quotation, It automates the processes of identifying potential suppliers, Supplier Evaluation, Supplier Quotation Evaluation,

Inventory Management
Inventory is always a key module for any organization. Here in DataNote, inventory module includes the Requisition, Material Issue (to shop floor / outside job-work party), Material Receipt from the shop floor. To get back the access raw material or any tool from the shop floor, Material Return will be required. Stock adjustment and stock transfer

QC & Calibration Management
DataNote ERP helps the QC department with its sophisticated Total Quality Management module. QC Module not only helps to capture the quality by control plans in purchasing and manufacturing but also provides real-time process capability index for quick review i.e through online quality control. QC in DataNote facilities are versatile and offer support for every phase

Finance Management
Finance Resource Management in DataNote gathers financial data from various functional departments. It generates valuable financial reports such as Balance Sheet, Trial balance, General Ledgers, Quarterly Financial Reports, Account Statements, Asset and Depreciation Management, Tax Management, ABC Analysis and many more.
