What is ERP system?

ERP is the acronym for Enterprise Resource Planning. It is a comprehensive software which is the core of a company’s operational needs like automation, flow of data accounting, back-office functions, inventory management, sales, marketing, human resource and much more. In simple terms, ERP is the life-line of enterprises. It helps in simplifying the processes of companies.

  • Manufacturing Management
  • Distribution Management
  • Financial Management
  • Reporting and Analysis

Why Industry Specific?
